Programs

Women Safe House Sustenance Initiative offers free comprehensive crisis services for survivors of gender based violence. We serve women and girls from Nigeria. Participation in our programs are free and confidential.

 

LEGAL SERVICES (LITIGATION) PROGRAM

We understand that navigating through the legal system can be expensive, difficult and confusing. Women Safe House provides legal services to women and girls experiencing gender based violence, stalking, human or sex trafficking in Nigeria

SHELTER (SAFE HOUSE) SERVICES

Women Safe House offers emergency shelter at an undisclosed location for women and girls fleeing gender based violence. The emergency shelter offers a trauma-informed supportive environment focused on healing. Survivors can access care coordination, counselling, healthcare and safety planning

COUNSELING PROGRAM

We believer that counseling can be instrumental in helping a survivor overcome that trauma experienced in an abusive relationship or violence faced to move forward. Counseling is available for survivors of all forms of gender based violence, sexual assualt, stalking or domestic abuse, whether it occured recently or in the past. Sessions are offered to women and girls as individuals or as a group  

COMMUNITY AND RELIGIOUS INSTITUTIONS EDUCATION & TRAININGS

We believe that knowledge of is key and that together with an informed community we can eliminate gender based violence against women and girls. We strive to engage, educate and inspire community leaders and members to join us in this belief. We offer a range of free trainings, campaigns, presentations and workshops to spread awareness about gender based violence agianst women and girls.

HealthCare Services

We understand that several women and girls in Nigeria do not have access to health care after experiencing violence. We provide immediate healthcare services to victims of violence to quick recovery from physical bruises and mental health stability
