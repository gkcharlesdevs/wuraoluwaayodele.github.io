3rd Annual Champions Unmasking Domestic Violence: A Masquerade and Casino Ball

  • Saturday, April 21, 2018
  • 7:00 PM 11:00 PM 19:00 23:00
  • Newport News Marriot at City Center (map)
  • Google Calendar ICS

Transitions Family Violence Services presents the 3rd Annual Champions Unmasking Domestic Violence......A Masquerade and Casino Ball on Saturday, April 21, 2018. The evening includes casino games, silent auction, live music and a tribute to those who have championed the cause of ending domestic violence.

Proceeds from the event will support programs/services for victims of domestic violence and sexual assault. With national conversation about the #MeToo movement, victims/survivors are coming forward to share their stories and receive help. This is your opportunity to stand in solidarity with #MeToo victims/survivors in your community. Your attendance at this event not only raises funds to support victims of abuse, it also signifies being a champion for change. Join us to unmask this issue of abuse in our community and raise funds to make a difference.

This event sold out last year, so get your tickets now!
